Locker Bangs: How They Work

Locker bangs are a way of life, an art if you will. They work because the killer is stuck in the animation of pulling out the survivor, and if timed perfectly, they cannot avoid getting blinded. Killers have an immunity to blinds while in this animation, so be careful not to throw the bang too early — otherwise it’ll be null and the killer won’t get blinded.

FAQ

Q: What killers are bugged?
A: Currently Demogorgon and Singularity cannot be blinded by locker bangs.

Q: Why do my locker bangs never work?
A: Coz ur a chud and don’t know timings properly yet — but that’s okay!

Q: Timing pointers?
A: Watch killer feet when pulling survivor. Count to 4 from red aura and drop just before 4 (1,2,3,3.5,DROP,4).
